Our History


Kay Nor transport was established in 2010 with one truck in Dollard des Ormeaux, within two years we expanded to have six trucks, servicing all of Quebec and the Toronto Montreal lane.

 

In 2015 we expanded into seven more trucks and started our dedicated route service servings the auto industries by transporting their parts through out Quebec.

 

In 2017 we continued to expand and acquired our offices and storage facility in Lachine where we operate our dispatching and warehousing operation.

 

With the hard work of our management and staff we are looking forward to expanding even more.
